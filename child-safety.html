Child Safety Standards

Little Genius is built specifically for young children, and protecting them is our highest priority. This page describes our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and how we keep the experience safe, as required by the Google Play Child Safety Standards policy.

✅ Little Genius contains no chat, no messaging, no user-generated content, no social features, no external links, and no ads. There is no way for a child to communicate with strangers inside the App.

Our commitment (zero tolerance)

  • We have a zero-tolerance approach to child sexual abuse and exploitation material (CSAE/CSAM).
  • The App does not allow users to upload, post, or share any content.
  • All content is pre-curated and reviewed by our team for age-appropriateness before release.

How the App is designed to be safe

  • No communication features: no chat, comments, messaging, or profiles visible to others.
  • No open internet: the App runs fully offline and does not browse the web or open external links.
  • Parental gate: account creation and deletion require an adult to pass a verification step.
  • No advertising: children are never shown ads or third-party content.
  • Curated content only: alphabets, numbers, language, values, stories, and games selected for young learners.

Reporting a concern

If you believe any content is inappropriate, or you have any child-safety concern, please contact us immediately and we will respond promptly:

  • Email: support@littlegenius.app

We will review every report, remove any content found to violate these standards, and where required by law, report CSAE to the relevant authorities (such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children, NCMEC, or local equivalents).

Compliance

These standards align with Google Play's policy requirements for apps that appeal to children. We review and update them as needed.
