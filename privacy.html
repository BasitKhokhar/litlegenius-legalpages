Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Little Genius mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information. Little Genius is designed for young children and is intended to be set up and managed by a parent or guardian. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with the Google Play Families Policy, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the GDPR provisions applicable to children (GDPR-K).

🔒 Summary: All information you enter stays on your device. The learning experience is fully offline. We have no servers; we do not collect, receive, share, or sell your data; and we use no advertising or third-party analytics/tracking. The app only uses the internet if a parent taps a link to open our legal/support pages in the browser, or for your device's own text-to-speech voices — never to send your personal information anywhere.

1. Information stored on your device

When a parent creates a profile, the following is stored locally on the device only:

  • Child's nickname — a display name you choose (a real name is not required).
  • Child's age band — used to tailor learning content.
  • Parent's email address — used only as a local login identifier.
  • A parent PIN — stored only as a salted, one-way cryptographic hash; the PIN itself is never stored.
  • Learning progress & star rewards — quiz results and earned stars.

2. How this information is used

  • To create and sign in to a local profile on the device.
  • To save learning progress and reward stars between sessions.
  • To show age-appropriate educational content.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not transmit any data off the device — there is no server or account in the cloud.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or show ads.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not create user profiles for advertising and do not sell or share data.

4. Permissions

The App requests only two standard Android permissions, and neither is a sensitive runtime permission:

  • Internet (INTERNET) — used only so a parent can open our legal and support pages in the device browser, and so your device's text-to-speech service can load voices. The App's learning content works entirely offline, and the App never sends your personal information over the internet.
  • Vibrate (VIBRATE) — used for gentle haptic feedback during activities.

The App does not request camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage permissions. Audio pronunciation is produced by your device's own text-to-speech engine (for example Google or Samsung TTS); only the on-screen educational text is read aloud — never your personal information. That engine is part of your device's operating system and is governed by your device manufacturer's privacy policy.

5. Children's privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Little Genius is a child-directed app. Because no personal information ever leaves the device, the App does not "collect" personal information from children in the sense of transmitting it to us or any third party. Account setup is gated behind a parental verification step so that a child cannot create a profile alone. A parent can view, change, or permanently delete all stored information at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data security

Profile data is kept in the device's app storage. The parent PIN is protected using a salted SHA-256 one-way hash, and the session token is stored in the device's secure keystore. Because data never leaves the device, it is protected by your device's own security (lock screen, encryption).

7. Deleting your data

You can permanently delete a profile and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account & Data inside the App. Uninstalling the App also removes all locally stored data. See our Data Deletion page for details.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
