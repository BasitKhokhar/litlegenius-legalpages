Welcome 👋

Little Genius is a 100% offline educational app for young children (Playgroup, Nursery & early primary). This center contains everything you need to understand how the app handles data and keeps children safe.

🔒 Little Genius's learning content works fully offline. It does not show ads, use third-party tracking, or transmit any personal data off your device. The only time it uses the internet is when a parent taps a link to open these legal/support pages.
