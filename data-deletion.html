How to Delete Your Account & Data

Little Genius stores all profile data only on your device. You are always in full control and can permanently delete everything at any time.

Option 1 — Delete inside the App (recommended)

  1. Open Little Genius and sign in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Tap “Delete Account & Data”.
  4. Complete the quick parental verification step.
  5. Confirm deletion. The profile, login, learning progress, and stars are permanently removed.

Option 2 — Uninstall the App

Because no data ever leaves your device, uninstalling Little Genius also permanently removes all locally stored data.

✅ What gets deleted: the child's nickname, age band, parent email, the hashed parent PIN, the login session, and all learning progress and star rewards.

What data is kept after deletion?

None. We do not operate servers and never receive your data, so there is nothing stored off your device to retain. Once deleted on the device, the data is gone.

Need help?

If you need assistance deleting your data, contact the developer and we will guide you through the steps. (Because we hold no server-side data, deletion is performed on your device.)
